US Flight Delay Analysis


Data Viz

Title: Analysis of Flight Delays in the United States (2017-2022)
Tools Used: Microsoft Excel, Tableau

Description:
Conducted a comprehensive analysis of flight delays across the United States from 2017 to 2022 using Kaggle's Flight Delay dataset. Data was prepared and cleaned in Excel, with visualizations and insights created in Tableau.

Key Objectives:

  • Identify the U.S. state with the highest total flight delay hours.
  • Determine the airline with the most significant delay hours.
  • Analyze trends in carrier-induced delays over the six-year period.

Email Marketing Campaign Dashboard


Data Viz

Title: Email Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard
Tools Used: Power BI, DAX

Description:
Designed a one-page interactive dashboard to visualize the performance of email marketing campaigns. The dashboard tracks key KPIs such as total emails sent, click-through rate (CTR), and conversion rate, segmented by country, device type, and campaign type. Built using Power BI with DAX calculations.

Key Objectives:

  • Visualize email campaign performance across countries, devices, and campaign types.
  • Enable marketers to identify top-performing countries and devices.
  • Support decision-making on targeting and optimization strategies.
